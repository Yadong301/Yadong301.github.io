Ya-Dong Wu

yadongwu@hku.hk

I am a tenure-track associate professor at the John Hopcroft Center for Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. Priorly, I was a postdoc in Prof. Giulio Chiribella 's group at the University of Hong Kong. I earned my Ph.D. from University of Calgary under the supervision of Prof. Barry Sanders. My research focuses on quantum information lying at the intersection of quantum physics and computer science. Recently, I am particularly interested in applying deep learning techniques for learning complex quantum systems from data.
